  1. What was your first impression of this site?
  2. How does this site organize the flow of information?
  3. Does the site structure make sense or does it confuse you? Why?
  4. Does the site graphics/layout/multimedia enhance the site or detract from it? Why?
  5. How long did it take for the home page to load? Was the load time reasonable?
  6. Was the text color and size readable?
  7. Did the site have a navigation bar or other navigation tool? Was the navigation well done?

Trader's Joe

URL: https://www.traderjoes.com/home

1. My first impression of the site was that it looks pretty clean and has a old time theme for its design. I did not like how the cookies notification and sign ups pops up immediately like all modern sites.

2. The front page has a button to see their products, and they have tabs on the left, with the first thing being Fearless Flyer, their newsletter. Everything else looks the same as most commercial sites, the location, About Us, Careers being on the top left and everything else on the botton of their website.

3. The structure makes sense, with their most important topic, being their products, be the first thing users will see. The site also follows standards that other sites use, so users can be familiar with the site, even if its their first time, since other sites have a similar structure.

4. I think the graphics enhance this site to be its own thing, even if the layout is very similar. Their aesthetic matches what they have at their store.

5. The homepage loaded in only a second, which is reasonable, since the home page needs to load fast for users.

6. Everything was readable. They used a white background and the font looked nice and fit their design choice.

7. The site has 2 main navigation tools. The first being the classic bar on top, but it just have their home page, a search function, a shopping list, and a hambuger button that shows all they have. The other is a side navigation bar, with only 5 things on it, being their newsletere, products, a discover tab, recipes, and their podcast.

8. The site uses mostly black and white, with accents of red. This continues all the way down until the bottom navigation section, which uses a very light green

9. The site responds well to smaller size, only losing the side navigation bar if it gets small enough.

10. I really like the side bar navigation and their theme, it has more personality than the corporate feel like amazon or walmart have with their website. The theme definitely helps this website stand out compared to other website.
